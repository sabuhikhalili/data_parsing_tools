Inflation Rate in Argentina increased to 289.40 percent in April from 287.90 percent in April, the highest increase since February 1991 and accelerating from 287.9 percent in the previous month. Inflation Rate in Argentina averaged 190.18 percent from 1944 until 2024, reaching an all time high of 20262.80 percent in March of 1990 and a record low of -7.00 percent in February of 1954. source: Instituto Nacional de Estadística y Censos (INDEC)

Inflation Rate in Argentina is expected to be 240.00 percent by the end of this quarter, according to Trading Economics global macro models and analysts expectations. In the long-term, the Argentina Inflation Rate is projected to trend around 30.00 percent in 2025, according to our econometric models.
